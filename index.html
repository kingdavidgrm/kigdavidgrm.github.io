King David is a 20 year old hip-hop/grime artist from Hackney, London who has spent most of his life in Dublin, Ireland. He considers himself an inspirational and uplifting musician who seeks to inspire young people with his personal experiences. He will be performing in his highly anticipated headline show at the Wiley Fox (Sound House) on the 20th of September 2017.
